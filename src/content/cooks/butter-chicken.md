ingredients:
  - 1 lb chicken thighs, cubed
  - 1 cup greek yogurt (marinade)
  - 2 tbsp garam masala
  - 1 can crushed tomatoes
  - 4 tbsp butter
  - 1/2 cup heavy cream
  - Garlic, ginger, chili powder to taste
  - Basmati rice to serve
steps:
  - Marinate the chicken in yogurt and half the spices, at least an hour.
  - Sear the chicken hard in a hot pan, then set it aside.
  - Cook down the tomatoes with butter, garlic, ginger, and the rest of the spices.
  - Blend the sauce smooth, stir in the cream, and add the chicken back.
  - Simmer 10 minutes and serve over rice.
