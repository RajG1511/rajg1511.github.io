## What it is

A Flask app that finds raves near me and adds them to my Google Calendar, because
manually transferring event flyers into a calendar is exactly the kind of manual
pipeline I refuse to run by hand.

Same discipline as the serious projects — ingest, normalize, sync — just with a much
better soundtrack.
